What the visuals say

  • The instruction vocabulary is tiny and imperative: put / pick / open / place over drawer / bowl / block / drawer. VLAs are tested on a narrow templated language distribution.
  • Standard LIBERO is saturated (OpenVLA-OFT 97.1%, π0 ~94%) — but the same models fall to 1–4% under a camera-viewpoint or position shift. Capability and robustness are nearly uncorrelated.
  • The same model is SOTA on one embodiment and near-zero on another: OpenVLA scores ~28% on SimplerEnv Google-Robot yet ~1% on WidowX (a known deployment artifact). Always report per-embodiment.
  • Never report standard LIBERO alone — pair a capability benchmark with a robustness one (LIBERO-Plus/PRO, COLOSSEUM).
