BEHAVIOR is the largest curated embodied-AI benchmark — 1,000 everyday household activities. Its 2025 NeurIPS Challenge evaluates whole-robot policies on 50 long-horizon tasks spanning real homes: cooking, cleaning, tidying, assembly. These are minutes-long, multi-room activities, and the leaderboard shows just how hard that is — the winner completes about 12% of full tasks.

Two metrics, two test sets. Full Task Success Rate (FSR) is binary — the entire goal must be met. Q-score awards partial credit for completed sub-goals, so it rewards meaningful progress and is the official ranking metric. Each is reported on a Public Validation set (self-reported) and a Held-out Test set (verified by the BEHAVIOR team). Tracks: Standard vs Privileged (privileged-information access).
