READING THESE NUMBERS
These are the only per-task success rates RoboCasa365 publishes — GR00T N1.5 after pretraining plus fine-tuning on 100% of the target data (Table 11), the best-performing configuration in the paper. They average ~65% across the 18 Atomic-Seen tasks.

High-precision, high-dexterity tasks anchor the bottom: TurnOffStove and CloseBlenderLid both sit at 37%, while a simple lever press, TurnOnElectricKettle, tops out at 93%.

Note this is a different, easier setting than the multi-task leaderboard below: with target fine-tuning, GR00T N1.5 reaches ~65% on these tasks; in the pure multi-task setting it scores only 50.7% on the same Atomic-Seen split.
